About us

UniGestionale is a team of young people that is completely focused on creating the largest world management community. We started in Turin and our mission is to close the gap between students, professionals and entrepreneurs. We believe that creating a strong ecosystem of people interested in management and entrepreneurship will train successful people enhancing a brighter professional career.
